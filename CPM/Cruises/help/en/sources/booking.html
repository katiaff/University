Filling your booking


Before starting to fill your booking, you have the option to load previous booking information you had, as long as it is for the same cruise. This can be done simply by clicking the "Open" button


To fill your new booking, firstly you must specify the date you wish to depart among all the available dates.


Then, choose a type of cabin. If you are 3 travellers or more, you can only choose a family room, unless you choose the "Extra Bed" option (only for children). However, you can still choose a family room if you are less than 3 travellers.


Next, enter the date of birth of all the travellers. Take into account that a single cabin can host only children, but there must be at least one adult in the trip.


Finally, choose your extras by selecting as many as you want and click on "Book".


Your booked cabin will be saved and added to your booking list.





When you click on any item in your booking list, you can see a brief description of the booked cabin.


You can also modify or remove any cabin in the list.


By choosing modify, you will load the information of the selected cabin. If you choose to book the cabin you are modifying, you will add a new booked cabin to the list. You will not lose the previous one.


If you wish to delete any cabin from the list, simply select it and click the button "Remove".



When you are done booking, you may proceed to the used data window by clicking the "Proceed" button.
