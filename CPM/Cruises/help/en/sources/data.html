Filling your user data


You must write your personal information in order to generate your receipt.


In the name and surname fields, you may not introduce numbers.


In the ID field you may introduce numbers and letters, at least 8 characters long


In the phone field you may not introduce letters, at least 8 digits long.


You can pay with credit card or with cash.




If you choose the credit card, you must fill in the extra data.


Below you can see the receipt that will be generated and stored in a file. If any detail in your booking is wrong, now is the last opportunity to change it.


You can introduce any important additional information we may need to know about. This information will also be reflected on your receipt.


To formalize your payment, click the corresponding button.
